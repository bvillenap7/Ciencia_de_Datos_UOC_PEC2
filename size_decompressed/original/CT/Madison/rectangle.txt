Box shaped object witnessed hovering above Madison connector.;2012-06-03 22:00:00;a few seconds;Box shaped object witnessed hovering above Madison connector. While driving north on the Madison connector, just outside of Hammonassett State Park, a box shaped object was seen hovering over the road approximately 25-30ft.  While passing underneath the object two read lights were observed on either side of the box.(like a set of red eyes)   After passing under the object it could then be seen in the rearview mirror of the witnesses vehicle.  The witness continued to watch it hover for about 2 more seconds before it swiftly moved up and disappeared into the sky. No sound could be heard coming from the object, nor was there any other like but the two read dots. Note: the object seemed to move slightly up and down as it hovered.
