BRIGHT OBJECT ENTERS VALLEY WHILE WE ARE ON THE PLAYGROUND.;1976-05-10 11:00:00;6seconds;BRIGHT OBJECT ENTERS VALLEY WHILE WE ARE ON THE PLAYGROUND. I WAS IN 5TH GRADE AT THE TIME. WWE WERE OUT ON THE PLAYGROUND PLAYING WHEN SUDDENLY A OBJECT AS BRIGHT AS THE SUN CAME INTO THE VALLEY AT A SPEED WHICH WAS PROBABLY THOUSANDS OF MILES PER HOUR.IT STREAKED ACROSS THE SKY SILENTLY AT 3 RIGHT ANGLES, REVERSED ITS SELF BACK OVER THE SAME ANGLES AND DISAPEARED. THE EVENT WAS SHORT BUT I WILL NEVER FORGET IT. IT WAS A CLEAR WEATHER DAY.COVELO IS ALSO CALLED ROUND VALLEY. AFTER THAT THERE WERE ALOT OF MILITARY PLANES[FIGHTER JETS]PRESENT.THE TEACHERS RUSHED US IN TO THE CLASSROOM AND PASSED OUT PAPER.SHE THAN INSTRUCTED US TO DRAW WHAT WE HAD SEEN.WE DID SO.TO THIS DAY I DONT KNOW WHAT HAPPEND TO THE PICTURES THAT WE ALL HAD DRAWN.IT STILL MAKES ME NERVOUS WHEN I THINK ABOUT IT, MY NAME WAS ON THE PAPER AS WELL AS MY FELLOW STUDENTS.
