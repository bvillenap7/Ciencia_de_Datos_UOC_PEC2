The three of us were lifting weights at a camp up in the Malibu canyons.  One of my friend saw it first and he yelled Look, look, the thing flew from our left and stopped right on top of us for a couple of seconds and continue on into the mountains.  It was quiet and didn't make one sound, not even a whoosh.   It was a clear and very, very beautiful starry night. ((NUFORC Note:  Witness indicates that date in April 1987 is approximate.
