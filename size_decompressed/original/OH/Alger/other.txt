BANK OF LIGHTS IN THE SKY.;2003-12-15 08:45:00;brief;BANK OF LIGHTS IN THE SKY. As I reached the hilltop and off to my left there was a bank of Lights very low to the ground about treetop height and a bout a quarter mile away.  It looked like stadium lights about 5 lights by ten lights . The lights appear to be yellowish to white about like the size of ordinary stadium lights would be.  This was in the country at the intersection of Rt. 309 and Alger Rd.  When i saw them it was about one second and they dissappeared. Instantly.  Very bright lights.
