Flashing Triangular red light formation seen moving across sky.;2017-04-11 21:07:00;1 minute;Flashing Triangular red light formation seen moving across sky. 2 people, looking at the moon from their deck, witnessed triangular formation of 3 round, flashing, red lights moving left, East to Southeast in the sky.   The lights slowly shifted from an isosceles triangle formation, to scalene triangle, then a straight line following each other.  All lights were blinking/flashing at differing times.   Attempted to photograph and record.  However the formation did not show up in any of the pictures or videos.   Lights moved out of witnesses' view after 1 minute.
