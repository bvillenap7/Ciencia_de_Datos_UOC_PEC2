Flying Saucer hovers above car.;1974-05-15 21:00:00;2 minutes;Flying Saucer hovers above car. This craft was typical flying saucer shape, it had bright blue lights flashing around the perifery one at a time. It hovered about 500 feet above my car for a few seconds, then in a split second it flew N.E. of me and hovered for a few seconds above the trees about 1/2 a mile away. Then it flew west of me about the same distance and hovered again. Then one more time S.E. of me before it took off towards space. ((NUFORC Note:  Date is approximate.  PD));45.2244;-122.8029
