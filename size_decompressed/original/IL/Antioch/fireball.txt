Orange fireball disks.;2014-09-20 20:00:00;5 minutes;Orange fireball disks 5 bright orange fireball type objects appeared at a low altitude over lake Marie in Antioch Illinois. They descended from the west almost out of nowhere, And continued to the east. At first there were only two.  One sped ahead,  Then came back to the other, then passed it again.  A few seconds later,  Anther appeared.  Heading from west to east.  Then another, and another. Both traveled from west to east.  They were very low, and made absolutely No sound at all.
