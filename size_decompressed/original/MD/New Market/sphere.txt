At approximately 10 PM three spherical orange flame-like objects were seen floating in the sky.  They appeared to be traveling in a horizontal line and were moving at a rather quick pace.  They flame-like objects were about at the same altitude planes fly but were definitely not planes.  Two of the three objects were further in the distance and disappeared once reaching the same point in the sky.  The third sped up and then disappeared at about the same point in the sky.
