Woman taking break from work sees a ball of light change color and then shoot off out of her sight.;1999-09-16 22:00:00;5 seconds;Woman taking break from work sees a ball of light change color and then shoot off out of her sight. At 10:00 PM on Thursday September 16th a woman was taking a break from work and looked up to the sky and noticed a ball of light rapidly changing color from blue to red to green.  After watching this object for only 5-6 seconds, it shot off out of the womans sight.  When asked the woman said the was not a shooting star as she has seen plenty of those before. She also stated that this object shot off very fast.  The object was seen in the Western Part off the sky.  The witness stated that the object was very low and had no sound to it.  The woman came forward with this story several days after finding out that a local paper ran a story on a UFO being reported the same day as her sighting.
