It was during the summer of 1975 and my Dad and I were going to the town of Dumas, TX, to see a movie.  It was still daylight as we were going to Dumas down a farm road.  As we came to the main highway, we made a stop and as I looked across the highway into an opened field I saw an oval shaped object about the size of a small house.   We sat there long enough for me to see two smaller oval dull blue objects that came from the west. They came to a stop under the first object and went into it.
