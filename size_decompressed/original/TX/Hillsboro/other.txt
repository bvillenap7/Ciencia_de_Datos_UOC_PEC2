Flashing star split into to separate stationary objects.;2014-09-21 00:45:00;15+ minutes;Flashing star split into to separate stationary objects. Noticed what appeared to be a flashing star in the northeast sky.  After a minute or two it split into to lights.  One appeared to be triangular in shape, the other was directly in front of/on top of it and was crescent shaped.   The both flickered orange, whit and some blue.  There also appears to be lights going back and forth between the two objects.  Both objects have remained stationary for about 20 minutes now.
