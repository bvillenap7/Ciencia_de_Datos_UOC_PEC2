Multiple sightings of lights in Franklin County, Maine.;2007-02-13 20:00:00;2 hours;Multiple sightings of lights in Franklin County, Maine. Many witnesses contacted Franklin County Dispatch Center regarding low flying lights, some strung in chains of 7 or 8, and an acompanying low jet engine sound.   These were spread out over a large geographic area and seemed to congregate around the area's mountains including Sugarloaf USA, a popular ski resort.
